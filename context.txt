What it is:
A mobile app that connects people with nearby, verified handymen for small home repairs (leaky taps, broken switches, furniture fixes) within 24 hours.

How it works:
Users post a job, see a fixed price, and book instantly. Handymen get paid per job, and QuickFix takes a small service fee.



Background: Former operations manager who noticed how hard it was to find reliable, fast home repair help.



Hook (Problem)
Finding a reliable handyman fast is frustrating and unpredictable.

Solution
QuickFix Home connects users with verified local handymen for small repairs within 24 hours at fixed prices.

Product Demo (Brief)
Show how a user posts a job and books in under 2 minutes.

Market Opportunity
Busy homeowners and renters in urban areas.

Business Model
Service fee from each completed job.

Why Us
Speed, transparent pricing, and trusted professionals.

Traction / Next Steps
